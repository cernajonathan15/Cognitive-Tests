Cognitive-Testing Builder

Build smart. Play fast. Stay accurate.

Your team will build cognitive tests and try to win the final challenge. A good test is not too easy and not impossible. The best test is hard, fair, playable, and measurable.

  1. Choose one final test-taker. They silently observe the other team build.
  2. Build 3 of the 4 possible cognitive tests. You may preview each test in Builder Mode.
  3. Rank your top three tests as 1st, 2nd, and 3rd choice.
  4. The final battle uses the overlapping task with the best combined ranking.
  5. Final scoring uses accuracy, reaction time, and difficulty. Reaction time only counts if accuracy is at least 70%. Difficulty only counts if the task was playable, meaning the other team scored at least 60% accuracy.
